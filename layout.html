Olympic games 1896-2012

The Olympic Games is an internationally celebrated sporting event that occurs every four years. It alternates between the Summer and Winter games and is regarded as one of the most prestigious global athletic competitions. Its roots trace back to ancient Greece, with the first Olympic Games taking place in Olympia in 776 BC. In the modern era, the Olympic Games include a wide array of sports and disciplines, ranging from track and field to swimming, gymnastics, ball sports and much more. Beyond athletic achievement, the Olympic Games serve as a platform for athletes to demonstrate their skills on a worldwide scale, and represent their home country against the world.
